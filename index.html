Poland Sock Poem

AllOfTheSocks

All of my socks have toes
Nine of my socks are differently colored
Eight of my socks makes half as many pairs
Seven of my socks i forgot to pack
Six of my socks are leopards and zebras
Five of my socks might have holes
Four of my socks are wet
Three of my socks are lost
Two of my socks are green
One of my socks feels like the desert inside
None of my socks are clean
